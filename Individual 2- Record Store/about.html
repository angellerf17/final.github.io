Who we are...

Founded in 2017, Seoulful Records is all about connecting one another. We thrive on community, creation, and the joy of listening to good music. We strive to bring the best of all genres to our friends and families. Housing oldies, newbies, and the goodies - rest assured you will find a record to go home with always. Come by for pop-ups, listen sessions, and recommendations. Can't wait to meet you and see what journey our records take you on.

We are always hosting events. Each Saturday is Ladies' Night - light refreshmants and wonderful ladies only records playing!
