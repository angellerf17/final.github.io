About this website:

This final project is a beginner-friendly website guide to understanding fragrance through the lens of a bartender - bringing mixology to the fragrence world. If you can describe a drink (fresh, creamy, smokey, sweet), you can describe a scent. The nose and mouth are connected!

On the Agenda:

  1. How top/middle/base notes work together
  2. How "feel words" lead to note families
  3. How to choose scents for seasons, moods, and occasions

How to use the site:

  • Start with Notes for the basics.
  • Use Cocktails to Scents page for fast pairing ideas.
  • Visit the Gallery and search by style.
