The Classic Martini is a symbol of elegance, grace, and refinement. Traditionally made with gin and dry vermouth, it's garnished with either an olive or a lemon twist. Contrary to James Bond, it is stirred, never shaken!

How to: Classic Martini

Ingredients:

  • Chilled Martini Glass
  • 2 oz Gin of choice
  • A few dashes of Dry Vermouth
  • A few dashes of orange bitters (optional)
  • Olives or Lemon Twist for garnish

Steps:

  • Chill glass with ice while preparing drink
  • Combine Gin, Dry Vermouth and Orange bitters (if wanted) into a mixing glass
  • Add ice to mixing glass
  • Stir. Stir. Stir. (for about 30 to 40 seconds) You do not want to over-dilute it.
  • Strain into the chilled martini glass.
  • Garnish of choice and enjoy!.
